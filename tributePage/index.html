Flying Spaghetti Monster For The Lulz

Flying Spaghetti Monster, the deity of what began as a parody religion and grew to become a social movement.

The adherents, who call themselves Pastafarians, purportedly number in the tens of thousands and are primarily located in North America, western Europe, Australia, and New Zealand.

The Flying Spaghetti Monster (FSM), which is said to be invisible, is depicted as a floating mass of spaghetti noodles with a large meatball on either side of its body and two centrally located eyestalks.
